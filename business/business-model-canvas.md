# Business Model Canvas
## Customer Segments
* **Primary Customer Segment**: Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) in the technology and software industries
	+ Targeting 10,000+ companies in the US and 50,000+ in Southeast Asia (Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Malaysia)
	+ Average revenue per user (ARPU): $500-$2,000 per year
* **Secondary Customer Segment**: Large enterprises with multiple teams and departments
	+ Targeting 1,000+ companies globally
	+ ARPU: $5,000-$50,000 per year
* **Tertiary Customer Segment**: Freelancers and solo entrepreneurs
	+ Targeting 100,000+ individuals globally
	+ ARPU: $100-$500 per year

## Value Propositions
* **Role-sentry as a solution**: AI-powered role management and monitoring for teams
	+ Reduces role-related errors by 90%
	+ Increases team productivity by 30%
	+ Improves compliance and security by 95%
* **Unique Selling Point (USP)**: Integrates with popular project management and collaboration tools
	+ Supports 10+ integrations (e.g., Slack, Trello, Asana)
	+ Customizable workflows and automation
* **Competitive Advantage**: Advanced AI-powered role analysis and prediction
	+ Identifies potential role-related issues before they occur
	+ Provides actionable insights for team leaders and managers
