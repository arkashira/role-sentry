# Customer Journey Map for Role-Sentry

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** IT teams or DevSecOps professionals encounter security breaches or compliance issues due to unauthorized access or role mismanagement.
**Friction Points:** Lack of awareness about role-based access control solutions, difficulty in identifying the root cause of security incidents.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, concern about security vulnerabilities, urgency to find a solution.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Educational content (blogs, webinars) on the importance of role-based access control, case studies of similar organizations.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to the product website or landing page.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** IT teams start researching solutions for role-based access control and come across Role-Sentry.
**Friction Points:** Comparing Role-Sentry with other solutions, understanding the unique value proposition, evaluating the cost-benefit ratio.
**User Emotions:** Overwhelmed by choices, need for reassurance about the effectiveness of the solution.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Detailed comparison charts, customer testimonials, free trials or demos, responsive customer support.
**Metric:** Number of downloads of whitepapers or case studies, sign-ups for webinars or demos.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Potential customers sign up for a free trial or demo of Role-Sentry.
**Friction Points:** Complex setup process, lack of clear documentation, technical issues during trial.
**User Emotions:** Excitement about trying a new solution, frustration if the trial process is not smooth.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Seamless onboarding process, comprehensive documentation, 24/7 support during the trial period, personalized demos.
**Metric:** Completion rate of the trial period, number of support tickets raised during the trial.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Successful trial completion and decision to purchase Role-Sentry.
**Friction Points:** Contract negotiation, integration with existing systems, training for the IT team.
**User Emotions:** Relief at finding a solution, excitement about implementing the new system, anxiety about the transition.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Smooth contract process, dedicated onboarding manager, training sessions, integration support.
**Metric:** Time taken from trial completion to purchase, customer satisfaction scores post-adoption.

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** Initial success with Role-Sentry leads to consideration of expanding its use within the organization.
**Friction Points:** Scaling the solution across different departments, ensuring consistent role management practices.
**User Emotions:** Confidence in the solution, desire to maximize ROI, concern about scalability.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Scalability options, additional features for enterprise use, regular check-ins to assess needs, upsell opportunities for advanced features.
**Metric:** Number of additional licenses purchased, expansion into new departments, customer retention rate.
