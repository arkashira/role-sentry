# Partner Targets
## Introduction
The following partner targets are identified for integration with role-sentry to enhance its value proposition and expand its reach.

## Partner Integration Roadmap
The integration roadmap is prioritized based on the potential value-add, ease of integration, and revenue-sharing opportunities.

### 1. **Slack**
* Free-tier limits: 10,000 messages/month
* Integration effort: M
* Value-add: Enhances incident response and notification capabilities for role-sentry users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, Slack has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 2. **GitHub**
* Free-tier limits: 500 MB storage, 100 MB bandwidth
* Integration effort: L
* Value-add: Streamlines DevSecOps workflows and enhances role-sentry's vulnerability management capabilities
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, GitHub has a developer affiliate program

### 3. **AWS IAM**
* Free-tier limits: 50,000 free requests/month
* Integration effort: L
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's identity and access management capabilities for AWS users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, AWS has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 4. **Okta**
* Free-tier limits: 1,000 MAU (monthly active users)
* Integration effort: M
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's identity and access management capabilities for Okta users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, Okta has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 5. **Datadog**
* Free-tier limits: 1 agent, 1 GB of data/day
* Integration effort: M
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's monitoring and incident response capabilities for Datadog users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, Datadog has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 6. **Microsoft Azure AD**
* Free-tier limits: 50,000 free authentication requests/month
* Integration effort: L
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's identity and access management capabilities for Azure AD users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, Microsoft has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 7. **Google Cloud IAM**
* Free-tier limits: 50,000 free requests/month
* Integration effort: L
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's identity and access management capabilities for Google Cloud users
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, Google Cloud has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

### 8. **CircleCI**
* Free-tier limits: 1,000 credits/month
* Integration effort: M
* Value-add: Enhances role-sentry's DevSecOps workflows and continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) capabilities
* Affiliate/revenue-share: Yes, CircleCI has a revenue-sharing program for approved integrations

Prioritization is based on the potential value-add, ease of integration, and revenue-sharing opportunities. Integrations with Slack, GitHub, and AWS IAM are prioritized first due to their high value-add and revenue-sharing potential.
